Dunamis Garbage Collection

DUNAMIS GARBAGE COLLECTION LIMITED is registered under the company With Patents and Company Registration Agency (PACRA). We are an environmental and waste management company on a mission to create a sustainable environment through consultations, environmental assessments and audits, waste recycling, advocacy, partnerships and collaboration


OUR VISION
To create a sustainable environment for all, to be a leader and preferred company of choice for environmental and waste management solutions in Africa.

OUR MISSION
✓ Our core mission is to achieve a sustainable environment in compliance with health, safety and environmental regulations in our operations by employing innovative environmental technology and services.
✓ Create value for our clients while employing local and international best practices for an efficient, sustainable and safe outcome.
✓ Accountable to our community and employees
✓ Protect our client’s right to privacy and confidentiality DUNAMIS GARBAGE COLLECTION LIMITED
OUR VALUES
We pride ourselves in creating sustainable solutions from the following key principles of being a company that:
✓ Leverages on global best practice for growth, development and sustainability to meet clients’ need
✓ Carry out our services with due care for the safety and health of employees, protection of the environment without compromising quality
✓ Professionalism and team work
OUR SERVICES
✓ Consultancy and Capacity building
✓ We provide advisory services to client, waste audits, environmental audits, environmental impact assessments and trainings in partnership with RICHFLOOD INTERNATIONAL.
✓ Recycling waste collection We collect recyclable waste from households, schools and organizations.
✓ Advocacy We partner with organizations and individuals in our advocacy program tag #lagosupcyclingtourism where we create awareness about waste management, upcycling and recycling.
